Privacy Policy

Effective: 25 May 2026  ·  Version 1.0

Your privacy matters to us. Inhale Mama is designed with privacy by default — the App collects no personal data, requires no account registration, and does not transmit any user-generated content to external servers.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide: Inhale Mama does not request or require any personal information — no name, email address, date of birth, or phone number.

The only user-generated content in the App is Custom Affirmations (available to premium users). These are stored exclusively on your device using local storage and are never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Information stored locally on your device: The following data is stored on your device only and remains entirely private:

  • Your premium unlock status
  • Labour surge session data (start/end times, duration, gap, sensation level, notes)
  • Custom affirmations you have created
  • App preferences and settings

This data is not transmitted to any server. It is deleted if you uninstall the App or clear your device storage.

Purchase information: If you purchase the 1-Year Access, the transaction is processed by Apple. We use RevenueCat to manage and verify in-app purchase entitlements. RevenueCat receives an anonymous identifier associated with your App Store account — not your name, email, or payment details. We do not store or process your payment information.

RevenueCat Privacy Policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

2. Information we do not collect

Inhale Mama does not collect, store, or process any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Location or GPS data
  • Health data or medical records
  • Device contacts, calendar, camera, or microphone data
  • Crash reports or analytics data
  • Advertising identifiers or tracking data

Inhale Mama contains no advertising and uses no advertising SDKs or tracking pixels.

3. Third-party services

RevenueCat is used solely to manage in-app purchase entitlements. It may hold a pseudonymous identifier and your purchase receipt. It does not receive your name, email, or payment card details. See revenuecat.com/privacy.

Apple App Store may collect data about your download and usage per their own privacy policy. We do not control or have access to this data. See apple.com/legal/privacy.

4. Children's privacy

Inhale Mama is not directed at children under 13. As the App collects no personal data, there is no specific risk to children's privacy from App use.

5. Data security

All App data is stored locally using your device's standard storage. We recommend using your device's built-in security features (passcode, Face ID, fingerprint). Purchase entitlements are managed by RevenueCat, which is SOC 2 Type II certified.

6. Data retention

  • All local data persists until you uninstall the App, clear App data, or delete it within the App
  • Uninstalling removes all locally stored data including surge history and custom affirmations
  • Your purchase entitlement can be restored after reinstalling via "Restore purchase"

7. Your rights (UK GDPR)

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, or restrict processing of your personal data. As Inhale Mama collects no personal data, most of these rights are not applicable in practice. You may also lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the effective date above.

9. Contact us

For privacy questions: hello@mamabreathe.co.uk

We will respond to all privacy enquiries within 30 days.
